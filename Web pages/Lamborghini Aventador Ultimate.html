Introducing...Lamborghini Aventador Ultimate...Shaping the Future!

Features:

Engine: 6,498 cm3 (396.5 cu in) V12 engine with 740 horsepower

Top speed: 350 km/h (217 mph)

Acceleration: 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph) in 2.9 seconds

Features: LDVA (Lamborghini Dinamica Veicolo Attiva, Lamborghini Active Vehicle Dynamics), hexagonal exhaust pipes, shark-shaped front end, interactive liquid-crystal TFT display, and customizable LED lighting system

Safety features: ABS, Electronic Brake-force Distribution (EBD), Brake Assist (BA), and Electronic Stability Program (ESP)

Overview:

The Lamborghini Aventador is a super sports car with a naturally aspirated V12 engine, carbon-fiber structure, and aerospace-inspired design. The Aventador has a top speed of 350 km/h and can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in 2.9 seconds. It has a seven-speed single-clutch automated manual transmission and an electronically controlled all-wheel drive system. The Aventador has a fuel tank capacity of 90.0 liters and a highway mileage of 10.70 km/L.
