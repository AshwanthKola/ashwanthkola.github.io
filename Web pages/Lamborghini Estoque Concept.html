Lamborghini Estoque Concept

Introducing...Lamborghini Estoque Concept...

Features:

Performance: 0–100 kmph in 3.30 seconds, city mileage of 2.50 kmpl, and highway mileage of 4.00 kmpl

Dimensions: Overall length of 5150 mm, overall width of 1990 mm, overall height of 1350 mm, and wheelbase of 3010 mm

Weight: Kerb weight of 1665 kg

Capacity: Seating capacity of 4 and fuel tank capacity of 100 liters

Brakes: Front ventilated and rear disk brakes

Wheels and tires: Alloy wheels with a tire size of 245/35 ZR18–335/30 ZR18

Comfort and convenience: Steering adjustment (rake and reach), steering mounted controls, rear AC vents, remote fuel filler, air conditioner, dual zone climate control, and power steering

Interior: Leather trim, large LCD display, high-quality audio, and rear-seat entertainment system

Overview:

The Estoque is a car with unique, unmistakable style. A long wheelbase, wide track and oversize wheels - combined with a low silhouette and a bold appearance - express the typical creativity of the Lamborghini style applied to a groundbreaking concept sedan.
