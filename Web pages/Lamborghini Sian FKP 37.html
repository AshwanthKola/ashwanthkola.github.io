Lamborghini Sian FKP 37

Introducing...Lamborghini Sian FKP 37...Build for Real!

Features:

Engine: 6.5-liter V12 engine with 602 kW (819 CV) of power at 8,500 rpm and 720 Nm of torque at 6,750 rpm

Transmission: Seven-speed Independent Shifting Rods (ISR) automatic transmission with an electronically controlled all-wheel drive system (Haldex gen. IV) and rear mechanical self-locking differential

Performance: Top speed of 355 km/h, acceleration from 0–100 km/h (0–62 mph) in less than 2.8 seconds, and acceleration from 0–200 km/h (0–124 mph) in 8.6 seconds

Braking: 100–0 km/h (62–0 mph) braking in 30 meters

Design: Wedge-shaped exterior with Y-shaped daytime running headlights and an active fixed rear wing with the number "63" embossed on its winglets

Body and chassis: Carbon fiber monocoque with aluminum front and rear frames

Wheels: 9”J X 20” H2 front rims

Steering and suspension: Electronic Stability Control (integrated ABS and TCS)

Overview:

The Sián FKP 37 is the first super sports car powered by a V12 engine and hybrid technology based on supercapacitors. Its powerful V12 engine, coupled with electric boost, creates an unrivaled gem of engineering and technology. Sián—lightning in Bolognese—is a name that captures the car’s true character, foremost its speed, which exceeds 220 mph (350 km/h).
