Lamborghini Terzo Mellenio Concept

Introducing...Lamborghini Terzo Millenio Concept...Design the Future!

Features:

Powertrain: The car uses an electrical propulsion system to power electrical components through cables. It also uses kinetic energy from braking to recharge the battery.

Design: The car has a modern Lamborghini design with Y-shaped headlights and taillights, a triangular front trunk, and a rear engine bay. The body panels are made entirely of carbon fiber, which is monitored by a health system.

Interior: Not many details are available about the interior of the car.

Dimensions: The car is 1143 mm (45 inches) high.

Overview:

Automobili Lamborghini looks to the future with a visionary approach, carrying our core values to extremes. Dictating the requirements of the third millennium, the Terzo Millennio combines energy efficiency and innovative materials to forge a path in the electric super sports car segment, guaranteeing the purest driving experience.
